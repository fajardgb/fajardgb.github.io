Research

Research Interests

I am interested in Computational Social Neuroscience. While computers have achieved superhuman performance in many domains including, chess, medical image diagnostics, predictive modeling, and driving, they still have a long way to go in understanding the complexities of the social world. I aim to study the mechanisms through which the social brain efficiently and effortlessly encodes the rich, dynamic, and high-dimensional data it receives from the social world.
